Local Resources (Kentucky)

Useful links and contacts to support farmers and community members:

  • University of Kentucky Cooperative Extension Service — Workshops, soil testing, and demonstration plots. (search: "UK Extension Service")
  • USDA Natural Resources Conservation Service (NRCS) - Kentucky — EQIP, CSP, technical assistance. (search: "USDA NRCS Kentucky")
  • Kentucky Soil Health Coalition — Soil health reports and local partnerships.
  • Kentucky Beef Network — Resources for rotational grazing and beef producers.
