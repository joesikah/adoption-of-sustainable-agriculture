Adoption of Sustainable Agricultural Practices

Summary: This site summarizes key findings on how Kentucky farmers are adopting sustainable agricultural practices (SAPs) to maintain productivity while protecting soil, water, and biodiversity. Topics include soil health, efficient resource use, cover cropping, integrated pest management, rotational grazing, and local extension programs that support adoption.
